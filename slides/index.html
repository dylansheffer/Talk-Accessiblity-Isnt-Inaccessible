What is Accessibility?

"The inclusive practice of
removing barriers
that prevent interaction with, or access to websites, by people with disabilities. When sites are correctly designed, developed and edited, all users have equal access to information and functionality."
Disabled World - Accessible Website Design Information
This definition is pretty good, but highlights a mentality I've noticed in the a11y world. (click) I'm not a fan of saying its the practice of removing barriers because it creates an us against them mentality. I believe the real root of web accessibility problems doesn't come from people not caring or purposefully putting up barriers. It's caused by people being generally unaware or by people who have been led to believe that it's too complicated or too insignificant to be concerned about.
"The practice of developing robust websites, so that people of all walks of life may have equal access to its information and functionality."
